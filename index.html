Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about our extension. If you have any other questions, feel free to reach out with email!

What is a bookmark manager? ▼

A bookmark manager helps you organize, save, and quickly access your favorite websites in one place.

Is my data synced across devices? ▼

Yes, your bookmarks are securely synced so you can access them from any device.

Can I import bookmarks from other browsers? ▼

Absolutely! You can easily import bookmarks from Chrome, Firefox, Opera, and more.

How do I organize my bookmarks into folders? ▼

You can create folders and drag-and-drop your bookmarks into them for better organization and easy access.

Is there a way to back up my bookmarks? ▼

Yes, you can export your bookmarks at any time to create a backup or transfer them to another device.
